Production

Operation: Wardrobe (2025)

Logline: When Avery’s favourite shirt goes missing, her best friend Casey plans an over-the-top heist to help her steal it back.

Description: Operation: Wardrobe (2025) was created as part of Digital Media Connecticut’s 2025 48-Hour Film Challenge. For more information on the challenge, please visit https://digitalmediact.com/48hourfilmchallenge/. Our team name was 2.5 Brains, 1 SD Card, and consisted of Carly Clarke, Austin Kipphut, and myself. On the project, I specifically wrote the screenplay, co-directed, and worked as one of the cinematographers.

Road Rager (2025)

Logline: A single father who’s barely making ends meet gets stuck in heavy traffic on the way to a crucial job interview.

Description: Road Rager (2025) was produced in collaboration with Jack Pia as part of the University of Connecticut’s DMD 1002: Foundations of Digital Media & Design II course. My contributions to the project included writing the script, storyboarding, and animating.

Beyond Borders: Immigrant Perspectives on Assimilation and Media Impact (2024)

No logline — view description below.

Description: This podcast episode was created in collaboration with Malana Johnson and Vicki Jade as part of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill’s MEJO 441: Diversity and Communication course, and was released as part of Season 5 of the Can You Hear Us Now?
