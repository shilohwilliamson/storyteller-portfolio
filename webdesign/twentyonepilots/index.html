TWENTY ONE PILOTS

About

Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. The band was founded in 2009 by Tyler Joseph along with Nick Thomas and Chris Salih; by 2011, those original members departed and Josh Dun joined, forming the current lineup.

They signed with Fueled by Ramen in 2012 and released their first label-backed album, Vessel, in 2013, which helped them break into the mainstream. Their 2015 album Blurryface propelled them even further, featuring the hit “Stressed Out,” which became their first chart-topping single.

Known for their energetic live performances, conceptual storytelling, and deeply personal lyrics, the duo blends alternative rock, hip hop, electropop, and indie influences. They sometimes describe their sound as “schizophrenic pop” to reflect this diversity. Their dedicated fanbase, known as the Skeleton Clique, has become one of the most passionate communities in modern music.
