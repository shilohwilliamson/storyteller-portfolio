Social Media Marketing

Stamford Board of REALTORS® 2025 Annual Meeting Recap Reel

Description: An Instagram reel recapping the Stamford Board of REALTORS® 2025 Annual Meeting, hosted at CoCreate. The event featured a panel discussion on Stamford’s economic future with Heather Cavanagh (President & CEO, Stamford Chamber of Commerce) and Leah Kagan (Director of Economic Development), recognition of REALTOR® of the Year Priti Kothari, and the installation of SBOR’s 2026 Board of Directors. I filmed and edited the recap reel for social media.

Stamford Board of REALTORS® × Paint the Town Pink – Happy Hour Recap Reel

Description: A recap reel highlighting the Stamford Board of REALTORS® Happy Hour at Farmhouse at the Crossroads, hosted in partnership with Stamford Health’s Paint the Town Pink breast cancer awareness initiative. I filmed and edited the vertical reel, showcasing member turnout, community engagement, and the fundraising efforts behind the event.

Realtor Safety Month – Realtor Safety Checklist

Description: A single-post graphic designed for the Stamford Board of REALTORS® Realtor Safety Month campaign. I researched member safety resources across SBOR, Connecticut REALTORS®, and NAR, then created an accessible checklist offering clear, actionable safety reminders for real estate professionals.

Realtor Safety Month – Local Realtor Safety Q&A

Description: A multi-slide carousel featuring local safety insights collected through Instagram/Facebook Q&A prompts and direct outreach. I gathered member responses, identified recurring themes, and designed a testimonial-style graphic that highlights real, community-sourced safety practices.
